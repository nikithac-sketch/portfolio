CLIENT OVERVIEW

About Hugosave

Hugosave is Singapore’s first Wealthcare® companion—a personal finance app designed to help users spend, save, and invest automatically and mindfully. Built on the core philosophy of mutual growth (Hu 互 meaning mutual, and Go meaning progress), Hugosave helps users cultivate healthy financial habits, grow wealth through gold investing, and achieve goals through gamified saving pathways.

Our collaboration focused on redesigning the micro-savings experience—Pots—transforming boring savings plans into a delightful, interactive visual journey tailored for Gen-Z and Millennials.

PANEL 01 // THE PROBLEM

Saving money today is a spreadsheet chore. Boring, cold, and easy to ignore.

PANEL 02 // THE SPARK

What if saving was a visual journey? A little train traveling down a road of goals.

PANEL 03 // THE LOOP

Each micro-deposit fuels your engine, unlocking exciting mystery milestones along the tracks.

PANEL 04 // THE WIN

Turning saving into an active game, making every milestone a reason to celebrate!
