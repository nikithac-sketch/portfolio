01 CLIENT UX CASE STUDY

Pots

Gamifying micro-savings through delightful milestones, visual pathways, and modular rewards.

CLIENT Hugosave
ROLE UX Designer
TIMELINE Jan - March 2024
TEAM MEMBERS 1 Product Manager,
1 UI Designer

Hugosave is Singapore’s premier Wealthcare® app designed to help users spend, save, and invest automatically. The platform empowers individuals to grow wealth by building healthy financial habits.

Aligning with this vision, Money Pots was introduced as a feature that allows users to budget some money on the side for their goals.

Everything was going great...

UNTIL

100% Created a Pot
68% Contributed to it
8% Completed their goal
PANEL 01 // THE PROBLEM

Saving money today is a spreadsheet chore. Boring, cold, and easy to ignore.

PANEL 02 // THE SPARK

What if saving was a visual journey? A little train traveling down a road of goals.

PANEL 03 // THE LOOP

Each micro-deposit fuels your engine, unlocking exciting mystery milestones along the tracks.

PANEL 04 // THE WIN

Turning saving into an active game, making every milestone a reason to celebrate!
