01 CLIENT UX CASE STUDY

Pots

Gamifying micro-savings through delightful milestones, visual pathways, and modular rewards.

CLIENT Hugosave
ROLE UX Designer
TIMELINE Jan - March 2024
TEAM MEMBERS 1 Product Manager,
1 UI Designer

Hugosave is Singapore’s premier Wealthcare® app designed to help users spend, save, and invest automatically. The platform empowers individuals to grow wealth by building healthy financial habits.

Aligning with this vision, Money Pots was introduced as a feature that allows users to budget some money on the side for their goals.

Everything was going great...

UNTIL

100% people who created the pot

To better understand the usage patterns, user interviews and app usage were studied.
This emerged 4 interesting archetypes who create a money pot:

37.84% The Architect
Amount + date ARCHETYPE 01

The Architect

Has a clear vision and a firm deadline. They’ve done the math, picked the date, and just need a system to execute. Every pot is a blueprint — structured, intentional, and on schedule.

Amount only ARCHETYPE 02

The Accumulator

Knows exactly what they want but isn’t in a hurry. They save toward a fixed number at their own pace — no pressure, no countdown. The finish line is the number, not the calendar.

Date only ARCHETYPE 03

The Countdown

Saving toward a moment, not a number. A trip, a wedding, a birthday — the date is sacred. They’ll figure out the exact amount later, but the clock is already ticking.

The Explorer

Something just felt right about starting a pot. No number, no date — just the intention to save. They’re still discovering what this goal will become, and that openness is exactly where to meet them.
